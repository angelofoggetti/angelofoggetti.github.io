Scientific Publications

Tuning Metaheuristics with Tree-Structured Parzen Estimator: A Case Study on Scheduling

Authors: Foggetti A., Nucci F., Papadia G.

Journal: Journal of Artificial Intelligence and Autonomous Intelligence

Status: Accepted for publication (May 9, 2025)

ISSN: 3049-2297

Summary: This study addresses the scheduling problem with sequence-dependent setup times by proposing an intelligent framework that integrates a Genetic Algorithm (GA) with automatic parameter tuning via the Tree-Structured Parzen Estimator (TPE). Tested on parallel machine scheduling problems, the method reduces completion times by up to 32.12% (14.87% on average) compared to exact algorithms under equal computational budgets. Applying TPE to Simulated Annealing yields inferior results (−1.10% to −13.23%), confirming the superiority of the GA-TPE approach. The proposed solution stands out for its scalability and direct impact on production efficiency and cost reduction.
